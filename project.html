HR Attrition Analysis Report

Data Cleaning (Excel)

In my analysis of HR attrition using Excel, my approach was structured and comprehensive. To begin, I initiated the process by meticulously cleaning and validating the dataset, ensuring data accuracy and consistency. This step was crucial in laying a reliable foundation for the subsequent analysis.

HR Attrition Analysis Report

I leveraged Excel's powerful features to create a Pivot Table, enabling me to swiftly summarize and examine the key trends within the attrition data. The Pivot Table allowed for easy segmentation and aggregation of data, providing valuable insights into factors contributing to attrition.

I took the analysis a step further by designing interactive dashboards within Excel. These dashboards not only visualized the data effectively but also enabled users to interact with the data, facilitating deeper exploration and understanding of the HR attrition patterns. This comprehensive approach, from data cleaning to dashboard creation, empowered me to derive actionable insights and make informed decisions regarding HR attrition within the organization.
